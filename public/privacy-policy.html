Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2024

Welcome to Task Fini. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This privacy policy will explain how our organization uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our application.

What Data Do We Collect?

Task Fini collects the following data:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile picture provided during Google sign-in.
  • Application Data: To-do items, projects, and related metadata you create within the application.
  • Gmail Integration Data: If you grant access, we process email headers (from, subject, date) to enable you to convert emails into tasks.

How Do We Use Your Data?

We use your data to provide and improve our services. Specifically:

  • To authenticate you and manage your account.
  • To store and display your tasks and projects.
  • To enable core application features, such as creating to-dos from emails.

Gmail Data Handling

Our integration with Gmail is designed with your privacy as a priority. When you authorize Task Fini to access your Gmail account, we do the following:

  • We only fetch a list of your recent emails when you explicitly open the "Import from Gmail" feature.
  • We only process the header information (subject, sender, date) to display a list for your selection.
  • We do not store the body content of your emails on our servers. The subject of an email is only saved as a task's title if you select it and explicitly click "Convert to To-do".
  • You can revoke our access to your Gmail account at any time through your Google Account settings.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Firebase (a Google service). We take all reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Your Data Rights

You have the right to request access to your personal data, and you may delete your account and all associated data at any time. Deleting your account will permanently remove all your tasks and project information from our servers.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.

How to Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please do not hesitate to contact us at privacy@taskfini.com.
